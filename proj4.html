KOI mil gaya!

A campaign for Kitchens of India

Kitchens of India is a ready to eat meal brand owned by ITC. This brand brought with it many challeges as the Indian audience does not prefer ready to eat meals.But, even when the ideation was not an easy process, it was an eventful adventure. The goal was to create a warm environment for everyone moving away from home for work or education through food. We wanted to create a personality that is amiable, reliable and altogether an wholesome experience with a tinge of humour. Everyone has one such person in their life whom they can rely on, theirpause button, their help in any situation,"Kash KOI meri madat kar deta." KOI are also the initials of this brand which makes it come together into a supportive vibe. Through this campaign, we intend to make the brand more approachable to the audience. In this fast paced busy world, this is their go to safe space, their KOI.





This is one of the first projects which I did with a team and I had so much fun as we shot an entire as film. It was an incredible experince!
